RETAIL
RETAIL

In the retail industry, one still has to seek for the optimal price, choose suitable assortment and plan the supply of goods in advance. Commonly, naive intuition and experience are sufficient to determine a price range for a good. Nonetheless, there is no clear answer for how to choose the optimal price? Also, there is no clear answer for how to construct product lines to avoid cannibalisation or spoilage. Sadly, business owners lose profits from inefficient planning and suboptimal pricing. However, recently retail has found a strong ally – digital sales data. neural-IO offers you some help with utilizing your data. Our retail solutions combine advanced techniques from econometrics and machine learning to answer the abovementioned questions and to increase your operational revenue.

AGRICULTURE
AGRICULTURE

Machine learning and Artificial intelligence have a huge impact potential in agriculture. In particular, satellite imagery, combined with drone imagery, could be used to monitor crop conditions. Moreover, the field data on crop conditions allows to improve plant care, by “personalizing” fertilizers and pesticides according with land conditions. Our team consists of the gurus in computer vision, who can work with satelite, drone, and photo imagery to perform pattern recognition. neural-IO will help your company to identify car numbers, personality of employees, their actions and current field conditions. Our solutions are already used on a daily basis for various purposes, for instance, to prevent theft on farms and to perform optimal fertilizer use, by leading Russian agriculture firms.

OIL & GAS
OIL & GAS

Machine learning is a natural necessity in the Oil & Gas industry since it helps to predict malfunctions of equipment. For example, analysis of data from various equipment censors allows to prevent failures of piston pumps of gas condenser. Rather surprising applications include: extraction of features of oil wells in order to estimate their average daily production volume and optimization of oil fractioning by forecasting chemical composition of the incoming resources. In light with the recent trend of declining prices on the oil market, increasing the efficiency becomes crucial. Our team is prepared to detect any major issues on your production process, which could be relaxed with the usage of machine learning. neural-IO will prepare a tailor-made solution to reduce operating expenses.

METALS & MINING
METALS & MINING

Metallurgical processes have not yet been fully affected by the recent rise in artificial intelligence. Besides, the benefits from innovating those processes can be easily quantified. Metals & Mining industry remains a fresh ground for machine learning. Common problems in metallurgical processes include equipment downtime, caused by malfunctions (which are accompanied by spoilage of resources), inefficient usage of expensive materials (for instance, ferroalloys), sub-optimal energy utilization. Successful, but still rare, cases of machine learning implementation allow to decline production costs on the magnitude of tens of percent. neural-IO offers a fully-specified solution, which uses equipment data to resolve the abovementioned problems and which can be tailor-made to your demands.

BANKING
BANKING

neural-IO focuses on crucial problems in the banking industry: personalizing multi-channel communication with clients and credit default prediction. SMS and notifications via bank’s apps constitute the most straightforward way to communicate something to a client. Moreover, individual web data allows to upgrade this communication to a whole new level –become a recommender system, creating additional value. neural-IO knows how to optimally personalize communication using all the available data. Estimating default probability is a classical task of banks. The goal is to choose the most precise and reliable prediction method. Our team is proficient in all the state of the art technologies used for default prediction and will help you to implement a best in practice solution.

LOGISTICS
LOGISTICS

Besides standard optimization problems given certain transportation constraints, logistics industry requires a lot of prediction. For instance, precise forecasting of weather conditions and the usage information on how weather conditions affected traffic in the past, can help to economize on travel time. The information from various trackers on vehicles can predict the time to potential malfunctions and can help with preventive maintenance of the automobile, which largely reduces both the transportation costs and time to cargo delivery. In general, machine learning technologies are becoming a beneficial innovation in the logistics industry. neural-IO team is ready to learn more about your logistics scheme and improve it with data analysis techniques.
