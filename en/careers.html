Careers

We value competency and value delivery. If you have an intitive sense with numbers with a good grip on fundamental mathmatical concepts, do work with us.

Currently, we are looking for Business Analytics Intern (paid, leading to job) to strengthen our Business Intelligence team.

Position: Business Analytics (INTERN)


Job details

  • No. of openings: 03
  • Part-time, 20-25 hours per week, Paid
  • 100% remote and flexible working hours schedule
  • Compensation/Bonuses based on work
  • Can increase to full-time if you are interested and a good fit for the position

Roles and responsibilities

    As an Intern, you will be involved in the following activities:
  • Build data analytics and visualizations using analytics tool (Tableau, Power BI, Python, R)
  • Working directly with Project Manager, CTO

    • Skills and Requirements:

    • Must have intuitive sense with numbers, analytics, and a good grasp of basic stats concepts
    • Basic understanding of any analytics tool as demonstrated through projects, portfolio, certifications
    • Ability to routinely, repeatedly, and reliably hit deadlines (very important)
    • Ability to work independently with little or no daily supervision
    • Communicate and cooperate with rest of team

    There is no specific criteria related to discipline, GPA etc. If you think you have an intuitive sense with data, and can deliver quality work, you should apply.

    When Does This Job Start?

    As soon as possible — We are targeting early/mid-November as a start date, but we will be flexible if you have life circumstances that prevent you from starting then (you have to be the right fit for the job, of course).

    What Happens After I Apply?

    After you apply, our team will be personally reviewing your application.

    After shortlisting, we’ll follow up with you over email, and set up a formal interview, normally within 3-4 business days.

    Later, you will be given 2-3 tasks of producing sample visualization/analytics based on a dummy dataset using any tool of your choice (Tableau, Power BI, Python, R etc - anything but Excel).

    We will be asking you to do this to check your:
    • Intuition with data
    • Approach in data analytics
    • Problem solving, formulating and presentation skills

    After selection, you will also be provided training/resources/time to sharpen your Business Analytics skills so that you are ready for real world dataset and tasks.

    We promise to be considerate and not “leave you on the hook” — if you are not right for the position, we will be sure to let you know within three weeks after the application period ends.

    Salary/Cash Compensation

    The position will start at the rate of PKR 10,500 per month. In addition, you may be eligible to be considered for bonus (upto 12,000 per month, depends upon the project work).
